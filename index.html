Here's what I do

My research involves handling terabites of genomic data that is generated after performing sequencing of liver cancers. The major focus of my research is on understanding the progression and evolution of hepatocellular carcinoma using whole genome sequencing data.

Genomics

Genomics is the study of the sequence of the letters in your DNA and how each string of letters passes information to help each cell in your body work properly.

Cancer

Cancer is the disease of the genome. By studying the cancer genome, we can discover what letter changes are causing a cell to become a cancer.

Data Analysis

Employing high performance computing and big data analytics on the Genomics Big Data in order to pinpoint "driver mutations" can lead to interesting insights into the development of cancer.

Towards Personalization

Genome guided patient treatment and diagnosis is leading to a paradiagm shift from "one drug fits all" to more individualized approach.
